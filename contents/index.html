Graphics Programming Virtual Meetup

Welcome, Computer Graphics lovers! We are a virtual meetup dedicated to graphics programming.

Since 2020, we have hosted hundreds of meetings almost every week.

We usually work through computer graphics books or articles on a weekly basis, though sometimes we have project presentations from members.
